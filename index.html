Case Study

Dept. of Homeland Security

UI Redesign

Problem Statement: The Department of Homeland Security is the parent agency of 22 other government agencies. The amount of data on the website can be overwhelming. The solution was a redesign of the website with an organized structure so that relevant information was brought to the forefront along with links to other agency websites.
Roles and Responsibilities: I was a member of two person team with the primary responsibility of performing a heuristic evaluation along with the Information Architecture responsibilities of creating a new site map and documenting the User flow.
Solution: Redesign the website so that relevant information is at the forefront of the site along with an organized structure and obvious links to other agency websites.
Tools Used: The tools I used included Adobe XD, Miro and FlowMapp
